### Learning Philosophy

I believe the best way to learn technical concepts is through **hands-on experimentation with short feedback loops**. My workshops and resources are designed to:

- Get you building immediately
- Provide quick wins and visible progress
- Make technical concepts approachable
- Show you how to apply skills to your actual work
